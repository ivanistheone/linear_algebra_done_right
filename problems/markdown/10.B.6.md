6 Suppose $A$ is a block upper-triangular matrix
\[
A=\left(\begin{array}{ccc}
A_{1} & & * \\
& \ddots & \\
0 & & A_{m}
\end{array}\right)
\]
where each $A_{j}$ along the diagonal is a square matrix. Prove that
\[
\operatorname{det} A=\left(\operatorname{det} A_{1}\right) \cdots\left(\operatorname{det} A_{m}\right) .
\]
